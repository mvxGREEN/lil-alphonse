in San Pedro, Ambergris Caye, Belize

Hol Chan

Hol Chan is located 4 miles southeast of San Pedro. It's like diving in your own aquarium. The ocean floor has a maximum depth of 30 feet, so both divers and snorkelers have the opportunity to swim in waters teeming with colorful tropical fish of all sizes and types along with beautiful coral formations. Divers can also enjoy the deeper waters outside the channel.

Shark Ray Alley

Shark Ray Alley is a must and it’s only a few minutes by boat from San Pedro. Hundreds of sting rays of all sizes congregate here accompanied by families of nurse sharks. With a depth of only 8 feet, the Sand Bar offers great photo opportunities.
