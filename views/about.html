About Lil' Alphonse

"Lil" Alphonse Granier is not your average snorkeling guide. Since childhood, he has been making friends with the sea life around Ambergris Caye, Belize.

No, really, they're friends!

Join Captain Alphonse and his first mate, Adolfo, and explore Ambergris Caye's underwater sanctuaries. No equipment or experience required! They will fit you with equipment on the boat and teach you to keep your mask crystal-clear. Flotation devices are also available.
